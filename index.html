    About Me

    I am Peiyuan Zhang, a PhD candidate in Economics at the School of Economics, Peking University. My research fields are primarily macroeconomics, heterogeneous agent models, mathematical and numerical methods in economics, and machine learning. I am also interested in applying machine learning methods to solve various problems such as time series, asset pricing, multi-agent reinforcement learning, and socio-economic simulations.
